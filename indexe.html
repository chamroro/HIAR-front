HIAR

Handong Institute for Arbitration and Reconciliation(HIAR)
solves domestic and international disputes through arbitration,
mediation, and reconciliation to promote sustainable peace and
cultivates students for dispute resolution through research,
education, and training, in accordance with the founding
philosophy of Handong Global University.

Founding Spirit & Vision

Established in 2021

Handong Institute for Arbitration and Reconciliation

The distortion of biblical creation appears mostly as disputes and conflicts through people or communities. The reason why Jesus came down to earth was to build peace by restoring the broken relationship between God and man. Peace was made between God and man through Jesus Christ Likewise, Handong Institute for Arbitration and Reconciliation(HIAR) was established for the purpose of building peace between people by solving disputes and conflicts through voluntary methods like arbitration, mediation, and reconciliation, which would ultimately lead to building peace between God and man.

HIAR aims to find a solution for settling disputes that can achieve true reconciliation and peace through biblical principles and the application of the positive law in a world filled with conflicts and disputes following the teachings of Christ to make true peace and reconciliation in this land.

“Blessed are the peacemakers,
for they will be called children of God.” (Matthew 5:9)
